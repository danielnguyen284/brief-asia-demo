ABOUT BRIEFASIA

Asia’s daily intelligence, reported by the people who live here.

BriefAsia is an independent newsroom covering business, finance, technology and the wider life of the region for the executives, investors and founders who cannot afford to be a day behind.

PART OF THE NETWORK

Published by Asia Press Centre Group

BriefAsia is a title of Asia Press Centre Group, a media network headquartered in Singapore and trusted across business, finance, technology, travel and lifestyle. That foundation gives our newsroom the reach and standing of a regional institution, together with the independence of a dedicated daily.

We report on Asia from inside Asia. The same editorial discipline that the Group is known for sits behind every story we publish.

Our mission

Asia is the defining story of the century, and far too little of it is told well in English. The reporting that matters is scattered across markets, languages and paywalls. BriefAsia brings it together so that busy readers across the region have one trusted place to understand what just happened and why it matters.

We hold ourselves to the standard of the best business press in the world, and we do it as a newsroom that belongs to the region rather than one that visits it. Our correspondents live where the news happens.
